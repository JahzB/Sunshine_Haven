OUR MISSION

Our mission at Sunshine Haven is to provide kids in poverty all around the world with the books for them so that they can explore the wonders of reading.

What Do We Do?

We travel around the world partnering with different organizations that deals with adolecence so that we can make sure to get these books to kids who actually need it

Why We Do This

Many children in our world today don't have the luxury to be able to read a book because they are unable to purchase a book. We at Sunshine Haven want kids to have this luxury the luxury to want to ready and enjoy reading.
